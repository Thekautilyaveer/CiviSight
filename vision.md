# CiviSight — Product Vision

> *Centralizing government compliance.* One hub that connects state agencies and counties to assign, submit, and track official forms.

This document captures the product vision, the problem, and the feature set behind CiviSight, sourced from [civisight.org](https://civisight.org). It is the "why" companion to `CLAUDE.md` (the "how").

## Vision & Mission

CiviSight exists to give state agencies and counties a single, live operating picture of compliance. Today, deadlines and forms are scattered across siloed tools, inboxes, and drives — and nothing gives leadership one view of where the risk is. CiviSight connects the parties that have to work together (state agencies, associations, and counties) into one system for **assigning, submitting, and tracking** official forms and reporting obligations.

The mission rests on three pillars:

1. **Visibility** — a unified operating picture across agencies and counties.
2. **Automation** — reminders, escalations, and AI-assisted form completion that remove manual overhead.
3. **Accountability** — role-based dashboards and auditable, on-demand workflows.

## The Problem

Government compliance workflows are fragmented:

- Deadlines and forms live across siloed tools, inboxes, and drives — leadership has no single live picture of risk.
- Staff burn hours on manual status tracking and spreadsheet reconciliation.
- Funding is lost when attestation and reporting deadlines slip through the cracks.
- Coordinating across many counties and many state agencies is slow and error-prone.

The stakes, as framed on the site:

- **$8.4M** estimated funding at risk annually
- **200+ hours** lost monthly to manual follow-up
- **159 counties** and **133+ state agencies** that must stay aligned

## Target Users

- State agencies and associations (e.g., the Association County Commissioners of Georgia — ACCG)
- County administrators and leadership
- County compliance staff
- State compliance coordinators

## How It Works

A four-step model:

1. **Partner with the State** — agencies bring their compliance requirements into a centralized system.
2. **Onboard all counties** — tasks, forms, and timelines are automatically assigned across every county.
3. **Finish faster, track automatically** — guided workflows with AI assistance; the state sees status in real time.
4. **Save hundreds of hours** — manual overhead drops, freeing teams to focus on governance.

## Features

### Compliance & Operations

- **Central Compliance Dashboard** — one live view of status, deadlines, and risk across every county, without manual spreadsheet compilation.
- **Global Task Creation & Tracking** — create a single assignment that applies across multiple jurisdictions, with clear ownership and real-time progress.
- **Role-Based Task Routing** — work is directed to the right person by role (not a generic inbox); comments, context, and handoffs stay on the task.
- **County Performance Visibility** — compliance risk indicators and priority levels surface where attention is needed.
- **Dynamic Contact Database** — living directories of county leadership and points of contact that stay current as staffing changes.

### Automation & Intelligence

- **Automated Reminder Engine** — proactive nudges before due dates, with escalations, to prevent missed deadlines and attestations.
- **Task-Level Communication** — every task carries its own conversation thread, preserving context and decisions inside the work item.
- **AI Form Assistant (FormPilot)** — guided help for long, complex government forms; automatically extracts and pre-fills required fields, saving an average of **15 minutes per form** and reducing error rates.
- **Smart Search & Filtering** — quickly find counties, tasks, and submissions; filter dashboards (e.g., to non-compliant counties).
- **Exportable Audit Trail** — timestamped, audit-ready history of actions, available on demand.

## Case Study — ACCG (Association County Commissioners of Georgia)

**Situation:** A new statewide compliance report became mandatory with immediate implementation. ACCG had to coordinate collection, validation, and follow-ups across **159 counties** while keeping deadlines clear, contacts correct, and the process auditable.

**Before:** Manual email broadcasts, spreadsheet tracking, and reactive follow-ups — creating handoff risk, visibility gaps, and weak compliance documentation.

**With CiviSight:**

- Role-based task delivery routed work to the correct county staff instead of generic inboxes.
- A real-time submission dashboard let leadership filter for non-compliant counties at a glance.
- Centralized task history survived staff turnover, eliminating restart costs and knowledge gaps.
- Automated reminders targeted specific owners.
- An exportable audit trail replaced manual evidence gathering with instant, audit-ready documentation.

**Outcome:** Moved from "chase a list, then chase people" to proactive, live status monitoring — with real-time visibility for leadership, fewer manual touchpoints, and compliance documentation ready on demand, mitigating the risk of missed deadlines and lost funding.
