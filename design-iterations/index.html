Counties Dashboard — 8 Layout Directions

Same data, same toolbar, eight different ways to present the county list. All deliberately compact and calm — simpler to scan, not bigger. Click any to open the live page.

Iteration 1

Data table

Familiar spreadsheet rows; numbers aligned for fast scanning.
Iteration 2

Compact cards

Small tiles, four per row, with a tiny stat trio per county.
Iteration 3

Progress rows

One row per county with a completion bar — progress at a glance.
Iteration 4

Attention first

Counties needing action float up; finished ones tuck away.
Iteration 5

Inbox

Slim two-line rows with a status stripe, like an email inbox.
Iteration 6

Summary strip

A thin totals bar for orientation, then quiet compact rows.
Iteration 7

Segmented

A segmented filter with live counts drives a tidy two-column list.
Iteration 8

Minimal

Just a name and one status — least to read, fastest to act.
